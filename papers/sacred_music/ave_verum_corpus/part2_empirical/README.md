## Nature of the Study

The present study carries the analysis of `../part1_manifold/` beyond the semi-synthetic. The dataset is derived not from the author's musicological intuitions but from the MIDI transcription of the published score, freely available through the International Music Score Library Project (IMSLP).

The study thereby permits a comparison between the structural quantities — the Lie derivative, the scalar curvature, the condition number of the decoder Jacobian — as computed upon the author's prior reading of the score, and the same quantities as computed upon the score itself. Where the two agree, the methodology of `part1_manifold/` is vindicated. Where they disagree, the sources of the disagreement are themselves of musicological interest.

## Relation to Part 1

The reader newly approaching this work is advised to consult `../part1_manifold/` first, in which the methodological framework is established in greater detail.
